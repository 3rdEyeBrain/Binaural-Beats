Binaural-Beats by GiorgioRegni

Brainwaves entrainment, auto hypnosis & lucid dreaming with an Android phone

Binaural beats stimulates your brain by sending special auditory artifacts directly into your headphones to produce subtle changes in behavior though entraiment of brainwaves frequency. This app can help induce relaxation, creativity and many other desirable mental states.
